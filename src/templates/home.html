The importance of personalisation

Students are more likely to have an effective learning experience when their environment is personalised. Resources, comments, suggestions and feedback that take into account the reality of each student are much more effective than those addressing an entire cohort. But personalisation has traditionally been based on highly detailed pre-conceived models of student behavior. Ontask offers an environment to personalise resources based on data collected from any learning environment.

The importance of feedback

Feedback is more effective when framed as a dialogue. But such dialogue will be highly situated in the personal situation of each student. How can we combine knowledge about the students with the need to have personalised interaction? Ontask will help instructors and students with the initial septs for this dialogue.

The importance of data

In the era of highly technology mediated learning, data is ubiquitous. But rather than an instantaneously benefitial feature, this data abundance brings a serious challenge to tranform data into actionable knowledge. It is not enough to have data, it is essential to put data to support a clearly identified objective in the learning environment. How can this support be clearly articulated? How can it be used in multiple environments? OnTask is the tool that realises this connection between data and its use to provide an scalable solution to learning personalisation
